About Us

About Us

HOAI GLOBAL CONCERNS was founded in 2003 with a single Mission: "To serve in the best interest of our clients, and ensure they are 100% tax compliant." Setting our eyes to the future with a Vision: "To provide value based consultancy, driven by best practices and to become a team of reliable, result oriented, dedicated, committed and responsible consultants."

Who we are

We are a Nigerian based financial consulting firm located in Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, Nigeria.Our firm has provided some excellent consulting services since inception in 2007 to a wide range of clientele. HOAI GLOBAL CONCERNS was conceptualized to advise clients on tax and financial-related matters. We are open to advising MDAs, corporate organizations and high networth individuals. Our track records for consistency, timely service, and issue-based expertise make it easy for us to overcome all challenges.

Our Business

We are focused on providing key financial solutions such as:
●Tax Services
●Regulatory Compliance Services
●Helping with Trusts
●Rental Property Income Tax

That’s why we have assembled some of the Nigeria’s leading professionals to meet the needs of organizations and individuals like you.
