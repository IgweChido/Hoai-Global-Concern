Consulting Overview

We are a Nigerian based consulting firm, located in the heart of Lagos Mega City.

Tax matters and government levies are some of the regulatory concerns of any CEO, and they are driving demand for proper internal control activities and thorough financial reporting processes to satisfy regulators and stakeholders.

Our firm was conceptualized to take care of these concerns. We have provided some excellent services to a wide range of clientele for over thirteen years. We advise clients on how to achieve their business objectives by reducing their tax risks and meeting their tax and other compliance obligations.

Our services include, but not limited to the following:

1. Tax Planning & Returns

2. International Taxation & Reporting

3. Tax and other Levies Advisory

4. Tax Compliance & Review

5. Tax Audit Monitoring

6. Ensuring clients do not pay unnecessary and unapproved taxes from local, state and federal governments.

7. Reviewing clients' bank statements periodically and engaging in forensic investigation for excess charges.
